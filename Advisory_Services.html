And, that’s where the concept of ‘Business Process Reengineering (BPR)‘ comes!

Why Business Process Reengineering (BPR)?

Business Process Re-engineering is defined as an approach that organizations adopt to bring overall transformation in their business methodology, making it efficient and modern. This can be measures to restructure formal procedures, change in existing processes and also overall changes in style of functioning and management.

In a line, BPR is an overhaul and rebuilding of the entire organizational framework for enhanced efficiency and high productivity.
Apart from the business side, BPR also brings in a culture within an organization in the form of thinking patterns from focus-oriented to process-oriented and eliminate non-value-adding elements.
Doing this gives organizations benefits of costs savings, enhanced quality, optimized delivery and improved end-user services.
